Ismael Sierra

About me

I am interested in Algebraic and Geometric Topology, specifically in questions related to homological stability: homological stability of arithmetic groups and moduli spaces of manifolds, cellular Ek-algebras, secondary stability phenomena, homology computations outside the stable range, and algebraic K-theory of fields.
